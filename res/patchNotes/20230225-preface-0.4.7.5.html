Hello once again!

First of all, I'm sorry about how long it's been since the previous update. Some personal issues which continued through December and January meant that I wasn't able to work on LT as much as I normally do, but that's over now and so the next updates should be released more regularly.

As for what's in this version, there are a lot of bug fixes and minor additions to the game. There's no large content additions, but I'm going to be focusing on that front for the next few releases, with more main quest content and side character content expansions being the focus.

There's also new art for three characters in this version. FriendlyAlienFriend (https://twitter.com/thealienfriend) has created art for Vanessa/Ms. Cunningham, while Davyn (https://twitter.com/davyndraw) has created art for both Kalahari and Kruger.

Thank you all for your patience and support during this long period since the last release. I'll do my very best to get the next version released within two weeks of this update!


Please note: You can transfer your saved games, exported characters, saved enchantments, and transformation presets from previous versions into new versions simply by copying the contents of your 'data' folder into the new version's 'data' folder!

Thank you all for playing Lilith's Throne, and a very big thank you to all of you who support development by reporting bugs, making PRs, or backing me on SubscribeStar! If you wanted to ask me any specific questions about the game, you can either find me on my blog, or on the Lilith's Throne Discord. You can find a link to the discord on my blog.
